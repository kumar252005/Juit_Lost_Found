Frequently Asked Questions

How long are items kept in the system?

All items and their associated images are automatically deleted after 7 days. This helps keep the system current and ensures we're only showing active lost and found items.

Is there any cost to use this service?

No, the JUIT Lost & Found service is completely free for all students and staff. Our goal is to help reunite people with their lost belongings without any financial barrier.

What should I do if I find someone's belongings?

If you find a lost item, please report it through our system with as much detail as possible. Include where and when you found it, and provide contact information so the owner can reach you.

How can I increase the chances of finding my lost item?

Provide a detailed description, include specific identifying features, upload clear photos if possible, and be accurate about when and where you lost the item. The more information you provide, the better your chances.

What types of items can I report?

You can report any personal belongings - textbooks, electronics, ID cards, keys, water bottles, clothing, bags, and other items commonly used by students on campus.

What if my item isn't found within 7 days?

If your item isn't found within the 7-day period, you can submit a new report. We recommend also checking with campus security and relevant department offices where your item might have been turned in.
